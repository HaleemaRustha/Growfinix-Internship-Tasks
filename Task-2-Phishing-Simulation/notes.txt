# SMTP Limitation Explanation

During the execution of the phishing simulation campaign in GoPhish, email delivery was not completed due to the absence of a configured SMTP server.

## Explanation

GoPhish requires an active SMTP configuration to send emails externally or to test recipients. In this lab setup, SMTP was not configured, which resulted in:

- No email delivery
- Campaign status showing 0 sent emails
- No user interaction metrics recorded

## Impact on Simulation

Despite the lack of email delivery, the following components were successfully implemented:

- Phishing email template design
- Landing page creation
- Target group configuration
- Campaign workflow setup

## Conclusion

The simulation successfully demonstrated the structure and setup of a phishing campaign. The absence of SMTP affects delivery only and does not impact the understanding of the phishing workflow or configuration process.
